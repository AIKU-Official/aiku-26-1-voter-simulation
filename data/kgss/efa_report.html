9. Group respondent pools — BNS direct resampling (Step 9)

BNS gives each agent a real KGSS respondent's factor-score vector (sampled with replacement) from its demographic×orientation cell, with a fallback chain — not Gaussian noise around a cell mean.

  • Respondents grouped: 1,009
  • Distinct full cells (age×sex×region5×orient): 208 (mean 4.9 respondents/cell)
  • Primary cells with n≥10: 33 → 45.4% of respondents land in a usable primary cell
  • Fallback (region5×orient): 25 cells, mean 40.4 respondents/cell
  • Fallback (orientation-only) pool sizes: {'C': 263, 'M': 379, 'P': 283, 'VC': 47, 'VP': 37}
